Agents

Some definitions I use...

Deliberative Agent

  • Contains an explicitly represented, symbolic model of the world
  • Makes decisions (for example about what actions to perform) via symbolic reasoning

Two important problems

  1. The transduction problem: that of translating the real world into an accurate, adequate symbolic description, in time for that description to be useful. (vision, speech understanding, learning, etc)
  2. The representation/reasoning problem: that of how to symbolically represent information about complex real-world entities and processes, and how to get agents to reason with this information in time for the results to be useful. (knowledge representation, automated reasoning, automatic planning, etc.)

Reactive Agent

(Rodney Brooks)
  • Intelligent behaviour can be generated without:
    1. Representations
    2. Abstract reasoning
    (of the kind that symbolic AI proposes)
  • Intelligence is an emergent property of certain complex systems

Hybrid Agent

... a mix of two (or more) subsystems
  • Deliberative System: Containing a symbolic world model, which develops plans and makes decisions in the way proposed by symbolic AI.
  • Reactive System: which is capable of reacting to events without complex reasoning

Critical thinking

(personal view) Agents can be useful to strengthen people's critical thinking.

What critical thinking is? Imagine you say there is no such thing as global warming. If there is evidence that tells global warming is the truth, and you say is not the truth, then you are in denial. Suppose you believe it, and afterwards you learn new evidence that falsifies it. If you can change your opinion, then your thought is critical. This ability to think critically helps us to interact effectively. Automated reasoning could be useful if its not biased.

Multi agent systems

Multi-agent systems can be
  • Competitive
  • Collaborative
  • Hybrids

In competitive negotiation, agents are self-interested and maximize their local utility. Cooperative setups try to maximize their global utility, they need to take into account activities of all the agents in the system.
